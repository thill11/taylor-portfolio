Process

I love to build. I try to build design on top of everything that I've learned or acquired in my life. I try to communicate a little bit of a guitar riff, a piece of poetry, or even lyrics and memories of my life into my work. Design is all relative to the situation, and I believe as one gets older the work starts to change along with the process, so in a way the work becomes the process and in the end comes a portfolio to die for.
